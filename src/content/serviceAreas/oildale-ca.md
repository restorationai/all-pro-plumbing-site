## Restoration emergencies common in Oildale

Oildale's housing stock tells the story before you even pull a permit. Born as a Standard Oil company town, the community north of the Kern River bluffs filled in with modest cottages and duplexes through the 1940s and 1960s, and a large share of those homes still carry their original galvanized steel supply pipe. Galvanized corrodes from the inside out — flow drops, pressure fluctuates, and eventually a fitting lets go without warning. Sewer laterals from the same era are often cast iron or, worse, Orangeburg: a compressed-paper pipe that was never meant to last past 1980 and is now collapsing under driveways across the 93308 ZIP code.

The climate compounds the problem. Summers here match Bakersfield's triple digits, and that heat hammers water heater anode rods, swells pipe joints, and pushes evaporative cooler pads past their useful life in a single season. The Kern River floodplain means the water table can be surprisingly shallow in low-lying parts of North of the River, which accelerates exterior corrosion on anything buried. Add the concentration of mobile home parks — where supply lines run under skirting and sewer connections are often original to the pad — and you have a community that generates a steady volume of urgent repair calls every summer.

## Services we provide in Oildale

**Emergency plumbing and leak response** — Burst pipes in Oildale's older cottages often hide inside walls or under slab-on-grade foundations before they announce themselves as a wet floor or a spike on the water bill. We locate the source, stop the water, and make the repair. **Drain cleaning and sewer repair** — Orangeburg and aging cast-iron laterals along North Chester Avenue and throughout Highland don't just slow down — they collapse. We camera-inspect, hydro-jet, and when the pipe is gone, we replace it. Trenchless lining is an option on laterals where the host pipe still has enough structural integrity to hold a liner.

**Repipes** — Whole-house repipe in galvanized-pipe homes is one of the most common jobs we run in the 93308 area. We replace galvanized with copper or PEX, restore full pressure, and pull the required permit so the work passes inspection.
