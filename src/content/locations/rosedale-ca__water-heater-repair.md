## Our Water Heater Repair Process in Rosedale

When a technician arrives at a Rosedale address, the first step is a full visual inspection: inlet and outlet connections, the pressure-relief valve, the flue or venting path on gas units, and the condition of the anode rod if the tank is accessible.
