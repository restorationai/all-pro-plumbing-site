## Our Sewer Line Repair and Replacement Process in Rosedale

Every sewer call starts with a camera inspection. We run a high-resolution drain camera through the line to locate the exact problem — whether that's a root intrusion, a cracked pipe, a belly, or a collapsed section — before recommending any repair. This matters in Rosedale's HOA subdivisions, where digging up a front yard without a clear diagnosis first tends to create friction with neighbors and property managers alike.

Once we know what we're dealing with, we present the options honestly:

- **Spot repair**: If the damage is isolated to a single section, a targeted excavation or a cured-in-place pipe (CIPP) liner can restore flow without replacing the entire run.
- **Trenchless sewer repair**: Pipe bursting pulls a new HDPE pipe through the old line, replacing it without a full trench. This is often the right call in Rosedale's finished landscaping and concrete-driveway lots.
- **Full sewer line replacement**: When a line has multiple failure points or has collapsed, open-cut replacement with new pipe is the most durable long-term solution. We pull the required Kern County permits and schedule the inspection before backfilling.

After any repair or replacement, we run the camera again to confirm the line is clear and properly graded before we close out the job.
