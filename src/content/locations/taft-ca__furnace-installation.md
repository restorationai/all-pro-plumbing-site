## Our Furnace Installation and Replacement Process in Taft

Every installation starts with a load calculation, not a guess. Taft's compact worker cottages have different heat-loss profiles than the newer tract homes on the edges of town, and dropping in a furnace sized for a larger footprint wastes fuel and causes short-cycling.
