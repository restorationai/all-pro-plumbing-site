## Our Drain Cleaning Process in Delano

Every drain call starts with a conversation about what you're seeing — slow drain, full backup, gurgling in a fixture you didn't even touch — because the symptom pattern tells us whether the problem is isolated to one fixture trap or sitting deeper in the main line. From there:

**Cable/rooter service** is the right first move for most residential clogs: grease in a kitchen line, hair and soap in a bathroom drain, or a soft obstruction in a tub trap. A motorized cable breaks through the blockage quickly and gets water moving again.

**Hydro jetting** goes further. A high-pressure water stream — typically 3,000 to 4,000 PSI — scours the pipe wall clean rather than just punching a hole through the clog. For Delano homes with years of scale and grease layered inside older pipes, hydro jetting restores closer to original flow capacity and pushes the next backup much further into the future.

**Camera inspection** is how we confirm the job is done and catch anything the cable or jetter couldn't fix — a collapsed section, a root mass that needs a different approach, or a belly in the line that will keep collecting debris no matter how often it's cleaned.
