## Why Tehachapi Properties See More Garbage Disposal Problems

The mineral content in Tehachapi's water supply — particularly in homes on private wells in Stallion Springs and Bear Valley Springs — leaves calcium and lime deposits on the grinding ring and impeller over time. That buildup is one of the leading reasons disposals start humming without turning: the motor is running but the plate has seized from scale accumulation, not a mechanical failure. A reset and a hex-key manual turn will free a mildly jammed unit, but when scale is the root cause, that jam comes back within weeks unless the buildup is addressed.

Older homes closer to Downtown Tehachapi — many built in the 1960s and 1970s — were plumbed with smaller-diameter drain lines and P-traps that don't always meet the clearance modern 3/4 HP or 1 HP disposals require. Installing a higher-powered unit in one of these kitchens without adjusting the drain configuration can cause chronic slow draining that gets blamed on the disposal when the real issue is undersized drain plumbing downstream.

## Our Repair and Installation Process in Tehachapi

Every call starts with a diagnosis before any parts are ordered or prices are quoted. For a repair visit, that means checking the reset button and thermal overload first, then testing motor draw, inspecting the grinding chamber for obstructions or corrosion, and looking at the mounting flange and discharge elbow for the source of any leak. A disposal leaking from the bottom almost always means the internal seals have failed — that's typically a replacement, not a repair worth making on a unit more than eight years old.

For a new installation, we measure the existing drain rough-in, confirm the electrical circuit (disposals require a dedicated 15- or 20-amp circuit under California code), and match the unit's horsepower to how the kitchen is actually used. A household doing heavy cooking needs a different spec than a weekend cabin near Brite Lake. We handle the mounting, the wiring connection at the switch, the drain plumbing, and the leak test before we consider the job done. Permits are pulled when the scope requires them.

## Local Note

One thing that comes up specifically in Tehachapi's mountain climate: disposal splash guards and mounting gaskets degrade faster here than in the valley.
