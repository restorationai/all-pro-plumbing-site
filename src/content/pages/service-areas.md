All Pro Plumbing Heating and Air dispatches around the clock — nights, weekends, and holidays included — from our Bakersfield base. Whether you're dealing with a burst pipe at 2 a.m., a furnace that quit on a cold January morning, or you're ready to schedule a water heater replacement at your convenience, the same team picks up the phone and gets a technician moving.

## Where we work

Bakersfield sits at the heart of California's southern San Joaquin Valley, and that geography shapes how we cover the region. The valley floor stretches wide and flat, which means our trucks can move efficiently across a large footprint without the mountain passes or canyon roads that slow response in other parts of the state. We serve Bakersfield and the surrounding Kern County communities — from the newer subdivisions spreading northwest toward Rosedale and Olive Drive, to the established neighborhoods east of Highway 99, to the agricultural and residential areas south of town toward Lamont and Arvin.

## Coverage by distance

Calls closest to our Bakersfield dispatch point get a technician on-site first. Communities within the immediate metro area — including Oildale, Greenacres, Rosedale, Riverlakes, and East Bakersfield — are our fastest-served locations. A step out from there, we regularly work in Shafter, Wasco, McFarland, Delano, Tehachapi, and Taft. For plumbing and HVAC jobs in those outlying areas, we schedule accordingly and give you an honest arrival window when you call rather than a number we can't stand behind.

One thing worth noting about Kern County: the hard, mineral-rich water that comes with valley living accelerates scale buildup in water heaters, softeners, and supply lines. Our technicians see that pattern every day, which means faster diagnosis and fewer surprises on the job.

For large-scale emergencies — a main line failure, a commercial HVAC system down, a significant slab leak — we'll discuss dispatch to areas beyond our standard range on a case-by-case basis.
