From a slab leak rumbling under your kitchen floor to an AC unit gasping through a Bakersfield summer that pushes past 105°F, All Pro Plumbing Heating and Air handles the full range of residential and light-commercial plumbing, heating, and cooling work. One company, one phone number — whether you're a homeowner in Southwest Bakersfield, a landlord managing a rental portfolio, or a small-business owner keeping a commercial space comfortable year-round.

## Emergency plumbing and drains

When a pipe lets go at midnight or a main drain backs up before a workday, you need someone who picks up — and we're available around the clock, every day of the year. Our emergency plumbing response covers burst and leaking pipes, drain cleaning (from a slow kitchen sink to a completely blocked main line), sewer line diagnosis and repair, and professional leak detection for the hidden leaks that show up as a wet spot on the ceiling or an unexplained spike on your water bill. If water has already spread beyond the pipe and soaked into walls or flooring, our sister company [ProRestoration Services](https://prorestorationca.com) handles the drying, demo, and rebuild so you're not coordinating two separate calls on a bad day.

## Water heaters and whole-home plumbing

A water heater that takes five minutes to deliver hot water, pops and rumbles with sediment, or simply stops heating is telling you something. We repair and replace both tank and tankless water heaters — and can walk you through the honest tradeoffs between the two before any work is quoted. Beyond water heaters, our plumbing scope covers full repiping for homes still running aging galvanized lines (common in Bakersfield's older housing stock), slab leak detection and repair, water softeners and whole-home filtration for the area's notoriously hard water, and fixture installation and repair throughout the house. Each job is quoted before work begins, so there are no surprises on the invoice.

## Heating and air conditioning

A furnace that won't fire on a January night and an AC that short-cycles on a triple-digit afternoon are both emergencies in their own right. Our heating and cooling work includes furnace repair and installation, heat pump systems, central air conditioning repair and replacement, ductless mini-splits for additions or rooms that never quite reach the right temperature, ductwork inspection and repair, and indoor air quality solutions — filtration, UV systems, and ventilation improvements for homes where dust and allergens are a constant fight. Whether you're replacing a system at end of life or chasing an efficiency upgrade, we'll size and quote the right equipment for your home's actual load, not just the unit that's easiest to pull off a truck.
