## Our Commitment to Accessibility

All Pro Plumbing Heating and Air is committed to making its website usable by everyone, including people who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, voice control software, or display magnification tools. We believe that access to information about plumbing, heating, and air conditioning services should not depend on a visitor's physical or cognitive abilities.

This site is built with the goal of conforming to the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA** — the widely recognized international standard for web accessibility. Those guidelines cover a range of recommendations designed to make web content more perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for users with disabilities.

## What We've Done to Support Accessible Use

Our web pages are structured with semantic HTML so that screen readers can interpret headings, lists, and landmarks in a logical order. Images include descriptive alternative text. Color contrast ratios are chosen to remain legible for visitors with low vision or color-vision differences. Interactive elements — forms, buttons, phone links — are reachable and operable by keyboard alone, without requiring a mouse.

We also aim to keep the language on this site plain and direct. Plumbing and HVAC decisions can be stressful; the last thing anyone needs is a page that's hard to read on top of a broken water heater or a failed AC in a Bakersfield summer.

## Known Limitations and Ongoing Improvement

Accessibility is not a one-time checkbox — it requires ongoing attention as content is added and the site evolves. While we work to maintain WCAG 2.1 AA conformance across all pages, some third-party content or embedded tools (such as mapping widgets or scheduling integrations) may fall outside our direct control. We review and address accessibility gaps as they are identified.

If you encounter a page, form, or feature that is difficult or impossible to use, we want to know about it.

## Contact Us About an Accessibility Issue

If you experience a barrier on this website, please reach out and describe what you were trying to do and what happened. We are committed to responding to accessibility feedback within a reasonable timeframe and to providing the information or functionality you need through an alternative means while any issue is being resolved.

## Third-Party Tools

This site may link to or embed content from third-party providers. All Pro Plumbing Heating and Air does not control the accessibility practices of those external services. If a third-party tool presents a barrier, let us know and we will do our best to offer an equivalent path to the information or service you need.
